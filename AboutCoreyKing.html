About Corey King

Corey has more than 20 years of experience in probate and guardianship law.  Her current practice focuses on probate, guardianship, estate planning, real property and related business matters.  In private practice Corey has represented many clients in courts throughout Florida, and has assisted many families with creating and changing their estate plans to maintain their assets and businesses properly.

Prior to her work in private practice, Corey worked for many years as a Senior Staff Attorney for the circuit courts in Gainesville and Fort Lauderdale, where she worked on thousands of probate and guardianship cases.  Corey's experience working on the court side of the law has provided her with a valuable understanding of the process and procedure involved in the specialized areas of guardianship and probate law.

- Estate planning, including wills, trusts, & powers of attorney
